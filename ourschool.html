school overview

Abdi Adama School, founded in 1998 E.C(2005 G.C.), is one of the leading schools in adama.

The school is known for providing a strong educational foundation and additionaly it places great focus on afaan oromo language and culture. unlike man y other schools, we make sure our students learn, speak, and value afaan oromo with confidence and pride.

Over the years, our school has expanded and now operates two additional branches — one located within adama city, and another in a nearby town. this growth reflects the trust our community places in us and our commitment to providing quality education to more families.

our team

Our teachers, administrators, and staff are dedicated, patient, and trained to support students of all learning levels. We believe that learning is more effective when students feel understood and encouraged, so our team works closely with each student to help them grow academically, socially, and personally.

our transport

We provide safe and reliable school transportation services across different areas of Adama. Our buses are regularly inspected, and each route is guided by trained staff to ensure students travel comfortably and safely every day.

uniform

Our uniform represents discipline, neatness, and unity. We encourage students to wear their uniforms properly and proudly, as it reflects their identity as members of Abdi Adama School.

extra curricular activities

At Abdi Adama School, education goes beyond the classroom. We encourage students to explore their interests, develop confidence, and learn teamwork through a variety of extra-curricular activities. Our programs include sports, arts, music, debate clubs, and science groups — giving every student the chance to discover their talents and grow socially and emotionally. We also believe deeply in community responsibility and compassion.

Our students take part in community service activities where they help and support those in need. The school organizes initiatives to provide meals, supplies, and assistance to needy community members, teaching students the values of generosity, empathy, and unity. We want our students to not only excel academically, but also to grow into caring and responsible individuals.

  • In Addition we offer: sports clubs (football, basketball, volleyball, athletics) arts, music, dance & theatre science & innovation club cultural heritage & language clubs debate & public speaking team

our curriculum

At abdi adama school, our curriculum is designed to build strong academic foundations while encouraging curiosity and creativity. we focus on core subjects such as mathematics, english, science, amharic, ict, and social studies, while also teaching problem-solving and communication skills.

Learning is interactive — students explore through hands-on activities, discussions, digital tools, and real-life applications. every student receives guidance and support to progress at their own pace with confidence and motivation.

our secondary school

Our secondary school prepares students for higher learning, future careers, and personal growth. learners develop critical thinking, leadership, teamwork, and self-discipline while strengthening their academic performance in all major subjects.

We guide students in choosing future paths — whether university studies, skilled professions, entrepreneurship, or innovation. our goal is to help students become confident, respectful, and purpose-driven young adults.
