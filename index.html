Excellence in Education

We nurture young minds from kindergarten through grade 12 with a well-rounded curriculum that balances academics, arts, and sports. our goal is to help each student reach their full potential and develop a lifelong love for learning. we focus on building strong foundations that prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.

Innovative Learning

Our programs combine interactive virtual classes with hands-on real-world experiences to make learning engaging and effective. students gain practical skills alongside academic knowledge. we embrace modern teaching tools and methods to keep education dynamic and relevant.

International Standards

abdi adama school follows global benchmarks to ensure our students are competitive worldwide. our curriculum, teaching methods, and assessment practices meet international quality standards. this prepares students to thrive in higher education and global career opportunities.

Welcome to Abdi Adama School! | Gara Mana Barumsaa Abdi Adaamaa Baga Nagayaan Dhuftan!

Dear parents, students, and residents of Adama City,

On behalf of Abdi Adama School, I extend a heartfelt welcome! I am Ato Girma Lemi, the founder and owner of this remarkable educational institution.

"To build the future of our children, we must plant the seeds of excellence today."

Abdi Adama School was established in 1998 E.C. (2005/2006 G.C.), and since that time, we have been diligently working to produce educated, competent, and patriotic citizens for Ethiopia. Every student has a story, and we are dedicated to filling that story with the light of knowledge and bright promise!

Abdi Adama School made history in Adama City as the first private school to open an instructional section in Afaan Oromoo. This crucial step ensures that children learn profound knowledge in their mother tongue and gain a deep understanding of their culture. Language is the bridge to culture!

We also offer classes in both Amharic and Afaan Oromoo. To deliver high-quality, modern education, we give special focus to Mathematics and English Language ‚Äî the key gateway skills for global opportunities.

The mission of Abdi Adama School is to combine modern knowledge with national values and pass this legacy to the next generation. Our school empowers students to develop comprehensive life skills, build self-confidence, and prepare to become agents of change for the nation.

Choose the school where your child grows with the best teachers, supported by modern educational resources, and nurtured with a hopeful vision for the future.

Abdi Adama School: Competent Generation for a Brighter Ethiopia!

Ato Girma Lemi
Founder and Owner

Our foundational campus, known for pioneering Abdi Adama School's commitment to core academic excellence, serves as a central learning hub for the wider Adama community.
